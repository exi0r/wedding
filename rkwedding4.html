Golden Hour Photography

Capture the Perfect Moment

Pro Tips

1

Best Time to Shoot

The golden hour occurs approximately 1 hour before sunset. For our April 19, 2026 wedding, that's around 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM.

2

Face the Light

Position yourself with the sun behind or to the side for that warm, glowing effect. Avoid harsh shadows by finding soft, diffused light.

3

Silhouettes & Backlighting

For dramatic photos, try shooting directly into the sun for stunning silhouettes and rim lighting effects.

4

Camera Settings

Lower ISO (100-400), wider aperture (f/1.8-f/4), and adjust white balance to "Cloudy" or "Shade" for warmer tones.
